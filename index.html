Projects

Smart Parking System

The Smart Parking System is an interactive web application that lets you reserve parking spaces in advance. You can choose from a variety of locations, prices, and availability. No more circling around or getting frustrated. Just book your spot and drive there.

The Smart Parking System uses Raspberry Pi and a camera to capture and recognize your license plate. It automatically checks you in and out, and calculates your parking fee based on your arrival and departure times. You can keep track of your parking fees and pay later with a simple click. The Smart Parking System is the ultimate solution for hassle-free parking.

Aqua Monitor

Aqua Monitor is an end-to-end product that monitors and controls water consumption with an interactive web application.

With this system, users can see their current usage, their usage history, and their usage trends with graphs and charts. They can also set limits and goals for themselves and get notified when they are close to reaching them. Water distributors can also manage their customers' water consumption and send them warnings or restrictions if they exceed their limits. They can also receive requests from their customers to extend their limits and approve or deny them. Aqua Monitor is a smart and fun way to save water and money, while also helping the environment.

Pothole Detection Android App

An Android application that uses smartphone's sensors to detect potholes while you ride your motorbike. It's a crowd-sourced data-sharing and gathering application that lets you contribute to a map of all the potholes detected. You can also see everyone's pothole data contribution, which is anonymous to protect your privacy.
